Rules:

This is an individual event. Google Classroom and whatsApp group of events will be created. In Quizophilic, there will be three rounds.

Round 1:
  1. In this round, a Google form will be provided to the participants.
  2. In the Google form, they will be asked questions related to Civil Engineering.
  3. You have to select the correct option.
  4. Top 16 will be promoted to the next round.
Round 2
  1. In this round, the participants have to join a Google meet.
  2. On Google meet, questions will be shown by presenting the screen.
  3. Each question will be shown for a few minutes. You have to write answers on paper and submit it on Google Classroom.
  4. On the basis of max correct answers submitted in less time, the participants will be promoted to the next round (3 teams).
Round 3
  1. In this round there will two sub rounds.
      Sub-round 1:
    • In this sub-round, Google form will be provided to you on which there will be picture of structure you will have to identify and submit it on the google form itself.
      Sub-round 2:
    • This sub round is Rapid Fire Round.
    • Participants will join Google meet on meet they will be asked questions and participants have to answer the question.
  2. The marks of the two sub rounds will be combined and the one who has higher marks will be declared as winner.
