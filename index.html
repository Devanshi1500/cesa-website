About CESA

Civil Engineering Student's Association - CESA is a departmental student's committee and has been organizing various lectures, conducting various events and carrying out projects since 2017. CESA has been successfully hosting STHAPATYA, a two day fest in month of February, since last 4 years and have been receiving huge response from all over Maharashtra and other states of India.





About VJTI

The Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute was established in 1887. Since the time of inception, VJTI has been playing a very vital role in producing quality engineers, introducing new programmes, and electives in emerging areas. The institute was granted financial and academic autonomy from June 21, 2004. The institute is reputed for excellent training in Engineering and Technology at Diploma, Degree, Post Graduate levels and Research. Presently VJTI offers B.Tech programmes in nine core disciplines of engineering, 17 M.Tech programmes, M.C.A and PhD programmes in various engineering disciplines.





About STHAPATYA

STHAPATYA is an annual technical fest hosted by Department of Civil Engineering, VJTI held under the aegis of the Civil Engineering Society in month of February since past four years. We organize various events related to the field of Civil Engineering to gauge students' ability to apply foundations of Civil Engineering into practice. Since its inception in 2017, Sthapatya has conquered a huge success in the field of engineering and corporate sector. Sthapatya 2020 was a huge success and since Sthapatya 2021 is going to be held virtually, it is going to reach even greater heights.

Career Prospects in Civil Engineering

Inauguration by Minister of Water Resources of Maharashtra

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to come to VJTI in order to participate in any of the events?

In awake of the ongoing pandemic, CESA has decided to go online and hence we will be holding STHAPATYA 2021 online and you can participate in any of the events from comfort of your home.

How to register for the events?

Open the event tab and go through the rules and regulations of the event. Then click on the register button and register for the event. For events where you have to participate in teams, state one team head with whom communication will happen. And then list remaining team members.

How do I get in contact with committee for other matters and queries?

You can click on the 'contact' tab or scroll to the bottom of the page and put down your concern or queries in there. We'll reach out to you ASAP.

What is the last date for registration of events?

There is no "last date" as such. (Of course before the events xD) But, the registrations will be closed as soon as we reach our upper limit per event. So, hurry up!
