Tendering.

Maximum 3 Members per Team (3 Recommended)

Government and big private organizations are one huge force that develops quite a great amount of infrastructure in the world. And one might wanna venture in their big projects, but then the question arises that how can one get his or her hand on the project. Well answer is tendering. The dynamics and tactics or getting tendered work is quite complicated and needs before hand experience in tender procedure. We introduce an event, the only one of it's kind in India, where participants will follow all the procedures of filling a tender and compete with the fellow participants to get the tender.

Rules:

  1. Event will last for two entire days. On the first day, the team needs to submit a plan and on the second day, cost estimation of respective plan in an abstract form.

  3. Tender notice and specifications will be given on the spot.
  4. All planning parameters will be considered.
  5. Marks will be awarded on first come first basis.
  6. Submissions will be accepted on Google classroom only. (You will receive the link to which in your email inbox once you register for the event.)
  7. Bonus points will be given on every addition.
  8. Decision taken by the judges and announced by CESA Team will be final and binding.
